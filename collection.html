— Inspiration —

Born from
Ancient Beauty

The Padma Collection is inspired by the lotus motif that has adorned Indian jewellery, architecture, and art for millennia. From the carvings of Ajanta to the jewels of Mughal courts, the lotus has symbolised purity, beauty, and the triumph of light.

Each piece in this collection reinterprets this timeless motif through VEDA's contemporary lens — using ethically sourced diamonds, heritage-grade 22K gold, and the meticulous hand-finishing that defines our atelier.

Like the lotus, true beauty rises from depth.

— Craftsmanship —

Every Petal,
Hand-Finished

The lotus motifs in the Padma Collection are not stamped or cast. Each petal is individually formed, hand-textured, and painstakingly assembled by our master goldsmiths. The result is a depth and warmth that no machine can replicate.

Our diamonds are ethically sourced lab-grown stones, set by hand in heritage-inspired bezels. Every Padma piece undergoes a minimum of 14 quality checks before receiving the VEDA hallmark.
