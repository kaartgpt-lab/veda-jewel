— Questions —

Frequently Asked

What is the minimum monthly amount?

There is no fixed minimum. We recommend starting with an amount that feels comfortable and sustainable — ₹5,000, ₹10,000, ₹25,000, or any amount you choose. The key is consistency, not the number.

Can I change my monthly amount?

Yes. Life is unpredictable, and we understand. You may adjust your monthly amount at any time. VEDA's eleventh-month gift will be calculated based on the average of your ten monthly contributions.

What if I miss a month?

We allow one grace month without any penalty. If you need to pause further, simply let us know — we'll work with you to find a path that works. Your savings remain safe and untouched.

Can I use my savings on any VEDA product?

Absolutely. Your accumulated balance — including VEDA's gift month — can be used on any piece from our collections, bespoke creations, or even as part of an Old Gold Exchange. No restrictions.

What if I want to exit early?

Your money is always yours. If you choose to exit before completing ten months, you'll receive your full savings back. The eleventh-month gift applies only upon completion of all ten instalments.

Can I gift a VSVP plan to someone?

Yes — and we love this. Many of our members start plans for their daughters, mothers, or sisters. We provide a beautifully designed certificate and a personal welcome for the recipient.
