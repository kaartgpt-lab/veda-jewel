The Story

The Sharanya Heritage Haar is a celebration of the temple motif that has adorned Indian shrines for millennia. Each panel is individually hand-formed in pure silver, with rubies nestled at the center and detailed filigree creating a halo of light.

This piece took our master artisans over 320 hours to complete — from the first sketch to the final polish. It is designed to become a family heirloom, carrying your story forward for generations.

Craftsmanship

Hand-crafted at the VEDA Atelier by master silversmith Ramesh Kumar and his team. Techniques used include lost-wax casting, hand-granulation, bezel setting, and antiquing.

Each temple detail is individually formed and textured to create a lifelike, three-dimensional effect. The piece undergoes 14 quality checks before receiving the VEDA hallmark.
